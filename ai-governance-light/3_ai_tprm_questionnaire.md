# AI Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM) Questionnaire

## Purpose
This questionnaire is designed to assess the security, governance, and operational risks associated with third-party AI tools and services. It helps organizations evaluate whether AI vendors can be safely integrated into SOC, cybersecurity, and GRC workflows.

---

## Section 1: AI System Overview
1. Describe the AI system or service being provided.
2. What primary functions does the AI perform?
3. Is the AI used for decision support, automation, or both?
4. Does the AI system operate autonomously in any capacity?

---

## Section 2: Data Handling & Privacy
5. What types of data are processed by the AI system?
6. Does the AI system process sensitive data (PII, credentials, logs)?
7. How is customer or organizational data stored and protected?
8. Is data shared with or used to train models outside the organization?

---

## Section 3: Security Controls
9. What security controls protect the AI system and its data?
10. How is access to the AI platform authenticated and authorized?
11. Are logs available for AI-related activity and access?
12. How are vulnerabilities identified and remediated?

---

## Section 4: Governance & Oversight
13. How does the vendor ensure human oversight of AI outputs?
14. Are AI decisions explainable or auditable?
15. How are AI errors, bias, or misuse identified and addressed?
16. Does the vendor support customer-defined usage restrictions?

---

## Section 5: Incident Response & Compliance

19. Does the vendor support compliance requirements relevant to the organization?
20. Are customers notified of material changes to AI behavior or models?

---

## Risk Evaluation Guidance
Responses should be reviewed to determine:
- Level of human oversight
- Data exposure risk
- Transparency and explainability
- Incident readiness

High-risk responses may require mitigation, contractual controls, or rejection of the vendor.
